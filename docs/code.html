Clue Detective (Android App)

Hasbro's classic board game Clue is a favorite of mine. So, I decided to make an android app for people who would like help while playing Clue with their friends.

Users can enter the cards in their hand or the cards which others show them throughout the game along with what other players are asking each other. Using all of this informaiton Clue Detective will calculate the probabillity of who is holding any of the cards in the game. For instance, the app may predict that there is a 20% chance that each of your 3 opponents is holding a particular card and that there is a 40% chance that the card is actually one of the three hidden cards.

The app is currently available on Google Play.
