WildPi - Recommendations

Fun and interesting things to do and see!

Programming

Trying to learn more about programming or need help learning a new technology? Here is where I go when I need help.

Stackoverflow

This forum has a great community of amateur and professional software developers who help each other with both the simple and complex problems they face in their projects.

W3 School

The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) provides W3 School as a free resource to developers to learn web technologies. Much (if not all) of the CCS and styling of this website is powered by their free CSS styling library W3.CSS

Thinking + Education

Looking for some recreational education materials or just want to watch/read something that will make you think? If so, then I'd recommend these ditigal watering holes.

CGP Grey

While Grey does not post often when he does his videos are always interesting. While I don't always agree with some of his philosophical positions or conclusions, he does a very good job of approaching common problems from a scientific point of view, and I have yet to not learn something from watching one of his videos.

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is a good resource for learning how to do math. I have used it for reviewing algebra and wrapping my brain around Integration-By-Parts

3 Blue 1 Brown

3 Blue 1 Brown is great! His "Essence of Linear Algebra" has done a lot to help me understand a subject that I have always felt was too complicated for me.

Relaxation and Recreation

The Internet is full of useful and thought provoking content, but it is also a place to have fun. Here's where I like to go after a long hard day at work.

HISHE

This is by far my favorite YouTube channel. How It Should Have Ended (HISHE) does a great job of exploring plot holes and incongrueties in movies and having fun with the characters we enjoy watching in our favorite movies.

Men of the West

Have you ever wondered what else happened in J.R.R. Tolkien's world of Middle Earth besides the War of the Ring. I have found good answers here on Men of the West.

Studio C

Do you like sketch comedy? Are you disappointed when a good sketch is ruined by an inappropriate comment or joke? May I present Studio C. They work to present wholesome comedy for everyone. Their older sketches are my favorite, so I recommend that you start there.
